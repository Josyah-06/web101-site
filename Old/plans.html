What’s Next

After graduation, I plan to pursue a career in Cyber Security where I can contribute to keeping online data safe from any outside sources and keep sharpening my skills both with my career and working with groups. For whatever job I get if it is a up and coming or a already made company I'll make sure to keep honing my skills and work as hard as I can so that I am a valued worker for whoever I work for.

Longer term, I’d like to become a higher up in a company and help mentor new developers, speak at meetups, and help the best I can so that the security for my company will be the best it can be. If I get the chace to be that higher up I will not let my company fall either i'll make sure to get a good supporting cast of workers so that it isn't just me doing the work so I can do more and make sure lots of people have the skills needed to be a good cyber security worker.
